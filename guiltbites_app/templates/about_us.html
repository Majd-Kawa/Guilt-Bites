About Us

Welcome to Guilt Bites! We create handcrafted treats made with love and quality ingredients.

It all started with a simple love for desserts… I've always enjoyed baking and experimenting with cookie recipes, trying to achieve that authentic, perfect taste in every bite. As my passion grew, I decided to learn more and took a course where I built a strong foundation. From there, I began creating and experimenting with unique flavors—not very common, but truly special in their own way. With time, the idea grew even bigger… So many people had been asking for my mom's homemade Ka'ak and Maamoul, and that's when we decided to add it to the menu—because honestly, its taste speaks for itself ❤️ That's how Guilt Bites became a blend of passion, creativity, and a warm homemade touch in every detail.
